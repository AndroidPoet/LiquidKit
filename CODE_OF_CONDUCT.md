# Code of Conduct

LiquidKit follows a simple standard: keep technical discussion respectful,
focused, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Critique code and decisions, not people.
- Keep issues and pull requests on topic.
- Assume contributors may be working across different platforms, tools, and
  time zones.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for it to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users who make the
project unsafe or unproductive for contributors.
